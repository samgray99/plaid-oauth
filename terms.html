Program Description

Daily Balance Reminder is a private, personal SMS notification service operated by Samuel Gray. The program sends one automated daily SMS message to a single pre-consented recipient containing shared household bank account balance information.

Message Frequency

Participants will receive no more than one (1) SMS message per day. Messages are sent once daily at approximately 9:00 AM.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on the recipient's mobile carrier plan. The operator of this program is not responsible for any charges incurred by the recipient's mobile carrier.

Consent

The sole recipient of messages from this program has provided explicit verbal consent to receive daily SMS notifications containing shared bank account balance information. Participation is strictly limited to one pre-consented individual within the same household as the program operator. No unsolicited messages are sent.

Opt-Out Instructions

The recipient may opt out of receiving SMS notifications at any time by replying STOP to any message received from this program. Upon receipt of a STOP reply, no further messages will be sent. To resume receiving messages after opting out, the recipient may reply START.

Help

For assistance, the recipient may reply HELP to any message received from this program, or contact Samuel Gray directly.

Limitation of Liability

This program is provided for personal convenience only. The program operator makes no guarantees regarding the accuracy, timeliness, or completeness of balance information delivered via SMS. Recipients should always verify account balances directly with their financial institution before making financial decisions.

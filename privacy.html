Privacy Policy

Program Name: Daily Balance Reminder

Last Updated: May 7, 2026

Overview

Daily Balance Reminder is a private, personal notification system operated by Samuel Gray for the sole purpose of sending automated daily SMS notifications containing shared bank account balance information to one pre-consented recipient within the same household.

Data We Collect

This program collects the following data solely for the purpose of delivering SMS notifications:

  • Bank account balance information retrieved via the Plaid API from linked financial accounts
  • The mobile phone number of the designated recipient

How We Use Your Data

Data collected by this program is used exclusively to:

  • Retrieve current bank account balances from linked financial institutions
  • Format and deliver a daily SMS notification to the sole designated recipient

This data is never used for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, trade, or otherwise disclose any personal data or financial information to any third parties. Data is not shared for marketing purposes under any circumstances.

Data Retention

Bank account balance data is retrieved in real time and is not stored beyond what is necessary to deliver the daily SMS notification. No historical balance data is retained.

Third-Party Services

This program uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Plaid — Used to securely retrieve bank account balance information. Plaid's privacy policy can be found at https://plaid.com/legal/
  • Twilio — Used to deliver SMS notifications. Twilio's privacy policy can be found at https://www.twilio.com/en-us/legal/privacy
  • Retool — Used to automate the notification workflow. Retool's privacy policy can be found at https://retool.com/privacy

Opt-Out

The designated recipient may opt out of receiving SMS notifications at any time by replying STOP to any message received from this program. Upon receipt of a STOP message, no further SMS notifications will be sent.

Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact Samuel Gray directly.
